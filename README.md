\# Student Performance Analysis



\## Overview

Analysis of 150 students across 6 subjects (Maths, English, Science, 

Social Studies, Computer, Hindi) to identify performance trends, 

subject-wise strengths and weaknesses, and top/bottom performers.



\## Objective

To evaluate overall academic performance, identify subjects needing 

improvement, and highlight top and bottom performing students for 

targeted support.



\## Tools Used

Microsoft Excel — Pivot Tables, INDEX-MATCH, LARGE/SMALL functions, 

COUNTIFS, Tie-breaker logic for duplicate scores, Data Visualization



\## Key Insights

\- Analyzed 150 students with an overall average score of 79.91

\- Achieved a 100% pass rate across all students

\- Computer (86.82 avg) and Science (85.04 avg) were the strongest subjects

\- Social Studies (79.95 avg) was the weakest performing subject

\- Top scorer: Rankini Patel (Roll No. 150) with 554 marks

\- Identified and resolved duplicate-score ties among top/bottom performers 

&#x20; using a custom tie-breaker formula for accurate ranking
